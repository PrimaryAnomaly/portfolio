## Problem

Bioregenerative life support experiments require precise fluid routing through microfluidic chips with leak-free connections at varying pressures and temperatures. Existing lab fixtures were ad-hoc and unreliable.

## Approach

Designed a custom chip holder and manifold assembly in SolidWorks with full GD&T dimensioning. The manifold integrates O-ring sealed ports, alignment pins, and quick-connect fittings. Prototyped using SLA 3D printing for rapid iteration, with final parts machined from PEEK for chemical compatibility.

## Result

Zero-leak performance at operating pressures up to 30 PSI. Reduced chip swap time from 15 minutes to under 2 minutes. Design integrated into the CubeSat biological payload thermal management subsystem.
